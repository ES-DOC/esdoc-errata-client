ESGF Errata Client documentation¶

The ESGF errata client, called esgissue is a piece of software that enables the interaction with the errata service. It can be used to create, update, close and retrieve issues. The client is basically aimed to be used by publishing teams that are notified of the existence of an issue regarding one or many datasets/files.
